Aid Distribution ⭐ Priority

Ayuda Distribution Schedule — Q1 2025

📅 Mar 20, 2025 👤 Capt. Reyes 👁 1,204

The Office of the Barangay Captain announces the first quarter distribution of the Ayuda sa Barangay program to support qualified residents.

📅 Distribution Schedule

Mar 28: Sitio 1 & 2 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
Mar 29: Sitio 3 & 4 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
Mar 30: Sitio 5, 6 & Remaining
Venue: Barangay Covered Court

Each household will receive:

  • 5 kg NFA Rice
  • Sardines (3 cans)
  • Cooking oil (1 liter)
  • Noodles (5 packs)
  • ₱500 cash assistance voucher

Requirements:

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Barangay Clearance or Barangay ID
  • Authorization letter if claiming for others

✅ Reminder

One (1) package per household only. Follow your sitio schedule.
