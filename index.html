Aid Distribution ⭐ Priority

Ayuda Distribution Schedule — Q1 2025

Posted: March 20, 2025
Barangay Captain Reyes
1,204 views
March 28–30, 2025

The Office of the Barangay Captain is pleased to announce the first quarter distribution of the Ayuda sa Barangay program. This assistance package is intended to support qualified residents in meeting basic household needs.

📅 Distribution Schedule

March 28 (Thursday): Sitio 1 & 2 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
March 29 (Friday): Sitio 3 & 4 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
March 30 (Saturday): Sitio 5, 6 & Remaining — 7:00 AM to 3:00 PM
Venue: Barangay Covered Court, San Isidro

What's Included in the Ayuda Package?

Each qualified household will receive the following:

  • 5 kg National Food Authority (NFA) rice
  • Sardines (3 cans, 425g each)
  • Cooking oil (1 liter)
  • Noodles (5 packs)
  • ₱500.00 cash assistance voucher

Requirements for Claiming

To receive your assistance package, please bring the following documents:

  • Valid government-issued ID (any one)
  • Barangay Clearance or Barangay ID
  • 4Ps beneficiary card (if applicable)
  • Authorization letter (if claiming on behalf of another)

Walk-in residents who are not yet registered in the beneficiary list are encouraged to bring proof of residency and may be accommodated on a case-by-case basis depending on remaining supplies.

✅ Important Reminders

One (1) package per household only. Please follow your assigned sitio schedule. Bring your own eco-bag or container. Strictly observe proper queuing and health protocols.
