Ayuda Distribution Schedule — Q1 2025

📅 March 20, 2025 👤 Barangay Captain Reyes 👁 1,204 views 🗓 March 28–30, 2025

The Office of the Barangay Captain announces the first quarter distribution of the Ayuda sa Barangay program to support qualified residents in meeting basic household needs.

📅 Distribution Schedule

March 28 (Thu): Sitio 1 & 2 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
March 29 (Fri): Sitio 3 & 4 — 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM
March 30 (Sat): Sitio 5, 6 & Remaining — 7:00 AM to 3:00 PM
Venue: Barangay Covered Court, San Isidro

Each household will receive:

  • 5 kg NFA Rice
  • Sardines (3 cans, 425g each)
  • Cooking oil (1 liter)
  • Noodles (5 packs)
  • ₱500.00 cash assistance voucher

Requirements for claiming:

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Barangay Clearance or Barangay ID
  • 4Ps beneficiary card (if applicable)
  • Authorization letter (if claiming on behalf of another)

✅ Reminders

One (1) package per household only. Follow your assigned sitio schedule. Bring your own eco-bag. Observe proper queuing and health protocols.
